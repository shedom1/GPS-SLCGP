April 2026

Mid-Atlantic

  • Kentucky — Public Health Dental Hygiene Teams RFA remained one of the clearest live procurement paths surfaced in April. Applications due April 24.
  • Maryland — April implementation update clarified April / May competitive timing, a combined transformation RFA path, and an April 28 pre-proposal conference. Keep as near-term pre-positioning rather than fully live procurement.
  • Tennessee — Funding Evaluation Consulting RFI moved into recently closed status after the April 17 deadline.
  • West Virginia — April verification confirmed the program page still routes users to a live vendor and subrecipient path through the state portal.

Northeast

  • Minnesota — April 23 review confirmed the HIT Advisory Services vendor RFP moved past its April 22 deadline, while formula-style provider funding materials remain live on the MDH RHTP funding page.
  • Nebraska — April 23 review confirmed multiple active RFAs remain in market and added a new RHTP 5.3 behavioral-health RFA for equipment and facility enhancements. RPM due April 24; HAI due May 8; RHTP 5.3 due August 31.
  • New Jersey — April directory update showed Community Health Workers closed while Doula Integration remains open. Doula Integration due April 27.
  • North Dakota — April 22–23 activity materially expanded with three newly posted opportunities under Make North Dakota Healthy Again: Zero-Hour PE, Community Gardens, and Community-Based Walking. All three new applications due May 22; Workforce Retention remains due April 30.
  • South Dakota — Active RFP rounds for digital health modernization and coordinated delivery remained in market through April review.

Southeast

  • Florida — April procurement update confirmed RHTP vendor RFQs are moving through the state term contract vehicle.
  • South Carolina — April posting opened four Year 1 grant tracks across Connections to Care, Leveling Up, Wellness Within Reach, and Shoring Up to Sustainability. Applications due June 1.
  • Georgia — April review kept the state in forecasted status but reaffirmed named first-year categories including Telehealth Enhancements, Workforce Retention Technology, and Telepods.

West

  • Montana — State moved into live April RFP activity, with additional opportunities expected over the coming months.
  • Nevada — Multiple live April funding paths remained open, including the Rural Health System Flex Fund and Rural Tribal Health Transformation. Flex Fund due April 30; Tribal funding due May 29.
  • Oklahoma — Community-Led Wellness Hubs Microgrants moved into recently closed status after the April 13 close.
  • Oregon — April posting confirmed the Catalyst Awards RFGP is live and added a vendor interest form for prospective partners. Proposals due May 26.
