Email-ready update

Subject

RHTP Daily Refresh: AR portal open; MD, WV, ND, SC updates to prioritize

Body

Team — today’s RHTP refresh adds several verified official updates. Arkansas THRIVE is now open, Maryland added a $6.3M rural primary-care RFA, West Virginia added a $29.5M Provider Productivity Support Fund, North Dakota posted new FAQs/handouts, and South Carolina posted FAQs for its four Year 1 grant tracks. Prioritize provider productivity, rural primary care, telehealth/RPM, EHR/interoperability, mobile care, and application support conversations this week.

May 2026

Mid-Atlantic

  • Maryland — MHCC released a $6.3M RHTP RFA to expand access to primary care in rural Maryland. Informational webinar is May 11; applications are due June 1.
  • West Virginia — Governor announced an additional $29.5M Provider Productivity Support Fund under Smart Care Catalyst, bringing current RHTP funding opportunities to more than $58M.
  • Delaware — Support Services RFP remains active, with questions due May 18 and proposals due June 22.
  • Pennsylvania — $25M Rapid Response Stabilization remains open through June 1 or until cap is reached.

Northeast

  • North Dakota — HHS posted May 8 and May 11 FAQs and TA handouts for active grant lanes; technology/data pillar remains coming soon.
  • Minnesota — ECHO webinar remains scheduled May 13; questions due May 22 and applications due June 10.
  • New Hampshire — GO-NORTH Evaluation Services RFP remains active through May 22.
  • Ohio — CHW Training and Technical Assistance solicitation remains open through June 15.

Southeast

  • Arkansas — THRIVE application portal is now open; applications are due June 12.
  • South Carolina — SCDHHS published official FAQs on May 8 for four Year 1 RHTP grant opportunities due June 1.
  • Texas — Rural Texas Strong and Infrastructure/Capital lanes remain active with May 27, May 28, and June 1 deadlines.
  • North Carolina — ROOTS Hub Leads remain the key regional partner-entry targets after May 1 selection.

West

  • Colorado — May 19 allowable-cost webinar remains the near-term engagement event.
  • Oregon — Catalyst Awards remain open with May 26 bid opening.
  • Washington — Mobile Health Assistance Van RFI remains active through May 31.
  • Montana — Advisory Council applications remain open through May 22.
