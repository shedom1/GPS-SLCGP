Prospecting guardrails, charter school note, and all-states scan tips
Guardrail: This page is for prospecting. It ranks source records using available Census place population and county SAIPE data. Final D-1 Rurality still needs USDA DLT map verification for each actual project site. K-12 district searches use the bundled ELSI/NCES 2025 district CSV with websites and # Schools; K-12 school-site searches use the NCES school layer.
Charter schools: Public charter schools can be screened here as potential K-12 end-user sites. They are not automatically eligible just because they are charter schools; the applicant must still be an eligible legal entity or part of an eligible consortium, operate or serve a rural community facility/residents, and have a DLT-eligible distance learning or telemedicine project.
All-states scan: Choose ALL — Target score scan in the State field or use the All States by Target Score button. For best speed, start with K-12 Districts, HRSA Healthcare, and Higher Ed / Technical; add K-12 school sites only when you need school-building-level records.
