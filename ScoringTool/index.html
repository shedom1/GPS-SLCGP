1) Build a Site List K-12 District + Schools, Rural Health, Higher Ed, or Manual

Important scoring note: Rurality cannot be fully automated from city population alone. USDA requires checking the site against the official DLT map for non-rural/contiguous urban areas. This tool calculates a provisional score and includes verification fields for the DLT map decision.

Healthcare workflow: Georgia is the default state. Leave the search box blank and click Load/Search Healthcare (HRSA) to load all HRSA health center service delivery and look-alike sites for the selected state, or type an organization, site, city, or county to narrow the results.

Recommended repeatable workflow: For K-12, search the district and import the district office plus all school locations. For healthcare, Georgia is the default state; leave search blank to load the selected-state HRSA list or enter an organization/city/county to narrow it. For higher ed, search the institution or upload a curated project-site CSV. Then click Enrich Census + SAIPE, verify all DLT map flags, and export the scoring worksheet.
