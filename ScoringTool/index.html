FY2026 RUS DLT Scoring Tool

Prospecting and pre-application scoring workspace for Distance Learning & Telemedicine projects. Add K-12 district schools, rural health sites, higher-ed campuses, or manual sites; enrich with 2020 Census place population and 2024 county SAIPE poverty; calculate provisional D-1 Rurality and D-2 Economic Need scores.
FY2026 GuidanceDeadline: 06/30/2026Grant: $50k–$750k

1) Build a Site List K-12, Rural Health, Higher Ed, or Manual

Important scoring note: Rurality cannot be fully automated from city population alone. USDA requires checking the site against the official DLT map for non-rural/contiguous urban areas. This tool calculates a provisional score and includes verification fields for the DLT map decision.
