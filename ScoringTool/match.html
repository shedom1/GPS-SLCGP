Match Source Reminders

Potential sources to investigate
Applicant cash; partner/end-user cash commitments; state or local funds; non-federal grants; philanthropy/local foundation support; Appalachian Regional Commission funds where applicable; and eligible in-kind items integral to the DLT project.
Watch-outs
Do not rely on E-rate funds, future revenue, already-installed equipment, vendor-donated match tied to purchases, or ineligible/non-integral items.
