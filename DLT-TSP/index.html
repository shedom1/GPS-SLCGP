What this tool checks
  • Grant request under/over FY2026 min/max.
  • 15% minimum match, based on grant requested.
  • Potentially ineligible or high-risk line items, including furniture, phone systems, EMR, admin/salary, recurring operating expenses, vehicles, construction/buildings/land, and broad miscellaneous bundles.
  • Cost-limit categories that commonly require scrutiny: technical assistance/instruction, video conferencing platform licenses, broadband facilities, warranties, site licenses, and maintenance contracts.
  • Budget structure risks: missing site, “All Sites” rows, vague descriptions, missing brand/model detail, and line items that appear bundled.
  • DLT use percentage below the predominant-purpose threshold.
