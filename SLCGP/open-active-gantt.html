Watch list: future-cycle signal, no current dated window

These states reference a future cycle or reopening, but the public page does not yet provide a dated window suitable for timeline placement.
  • Pennsylvania — Pennsylvania's official cybersecurity grant page says local governments will be able to apply to the Commonwealth to participate in cybersecurity projects identified by the committee, cannot apply directly to the federal government, and should sign up for updates. The page says more information to apply for 2025 funding will be provided later. Source
  • Montana — Montana says its most recent application window closed January 9, 2026 and that the next application window will open by late spring 2026. The official page does not currently post a specific opening date. Source
  • Oregon — Oregon's official SLCGP page says the funding opportunity is temporarily closed as of April 1, 2026 and that new applications are anticipated in Summer 2026. The page continues to direct applicants to Oregon's Cybersecurity Plan and the SLCGP administrative email for questions. Source
