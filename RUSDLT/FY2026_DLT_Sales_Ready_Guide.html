What DLT Funds

DLT supports telecommunications-enabled information, audio/video equipment, and advanced technologies that improve rural access to education, training, and healthcare resources.

Distance Learning

Interactive classrooms, CTE/workforce instruction, higher-ed-to-K-12 delivery, training simulators, and collaborative teaching environments.

Telemedicine

Telehealth carts, clinical video rooms, remote specialty consults, behavioral health access, patient monitoring, and diagnostic image transmission.

Network Components

Computer hardware/software, networking components, inside wiring, telecommunications terminal equipment, and limited broadband facilities.

Project Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity software can be eligible when directly related to the funded DLT project.
