2) Keep your existing tracker folders in place:
- /RHTP/
- /SLCGP/
- /SLCGPv2/
- /FedDash/
- /RUS-DLT/
- /ECYPP/
- /eRate-471/
- /eRate-470/

3) In the linked Google Sheet, open Extensions > Apps Script
- Paste in Code.gs if needed
- Save
- Deploy as a Web App
- Execute as: Me
- Who has access: Anyone
